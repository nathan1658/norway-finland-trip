  • My Aurora Forecast & Alerts (App Store)

  • NightCap Pro (long exposure photography)

🌌 Best Aurora Spots

  • Tromsø: Telegrafbukta, Prestvannet Lake
  • Rovaniemi: Aurora Hill Resort hilltop
  • Levi: Fell slopes after midnight
  • Need: Clear sky, KP index ≥ 2, dark location

⏰ Time Zones

Norway (CET) = HKT − 7 hrs
Finland (EET) = HKT − 6 hrs
+1 hour when you cross into Finland on 21 Mar.

💰 Currency

  • Norway 🇳🇴 — NOK (Norwegian Krone)
  • Finland 🇫🇮 — EUR (Euro)
  • Cards widely accepted everywhere
  • Oslo & Helsinki are cashless-friendly

🧥 Packing Essentials

  • Thermal base layers (merino wool)
  • Waterproof outer layer
  • Insulated boots (−20°C rated)
  • Hand warmers, balaclava, ski goggles
  • Microspikes for icy paths

🚌 Getting Around

  • Oslo: Tram + Metro (Ruter app)
  • Lofoten: Rental car essential
  • Tromsø: Walking + local bus
  • Rovaniemi/Levi: Bus (Matkahuolto app)
  • Helsinki: Tram + Metro (HSL app)
